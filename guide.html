The PTP™ Guide

Inside the Guide

The PTP Guide includes over a dozen sections chosen specifically to help couples prepare for the relational, emotional, and logistical changes of early parenthood. Each section is designed to be completed by the couple — individually or together — and then discussed as a partnership.

01

Shared Family Values

Take time to discuss prompts about your shared values as you revisit the foundation for your growing family.

02

First 30 Days

Consider the unique priorities each partner will have during the first 30 days.

03

Support Network

Discuss who will make up your support network, and whether there are any gaps.

04

Mental Load Map

Consider what behind-the-scenes tasks still remain before the arrival of your newborn.

05

Overnight Strategy

Discuss ways you can divide and conquer the night shift together.

06

Visitor Plan

Decide what your plans and boundaries are for visitors during this time.

07

Staying Connected

Generate a plan for staying connected with each other during the first few months of big changes.

08

Check-Ins

Revisit your original plans and make changes as your strategies and partnership evolve.
