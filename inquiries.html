About the author

Tayler Bungo Colón  MPH, CEC

Tayler Bungo Colón, MPH, CEC, holds degrees in psychology, neuroscience, and public health, with over a decade of experience as a health policy professional. She serves as Director of a team that manages multi-million-dollar healthcare transformation programs aimed at improving access, quality, and equity in patient care while lowering costs.

Her deep expertise in teaching, coaching, adult learning, health equity, and maternal health — combined with the birth of her son in 2025 — led her to take a personal tool she used during her own postpartum journey to the next level. She hopes other couples can benefit from the Partners Through Postpartum™ program as she did with her partner, Will, and son, Santo.
