About PTP™

Partners Through Postpartum™ is a structured preparation framework, designed to help couples prepare their relationship for the postpartum period.

It was developed in response to the well-documented relationship strain during the transition to parenthood — a time of heightened stress, new routines, and shifting roles. Rather than waiting for friction to surface, PTP gives partners an opportunity to reconnect and discuss shared expectations before the newborn arrives.
